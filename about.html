Our Story

About Millbrook Manor

Welcome to Millbrook Manor Estate! The Estate consists of 5 individual houses on lakefront property in beautiful Lake George, New York. Each house has its own unique accommodations and special touch. The entire Estate is available for rent from May through September. It's perfect for family vacations, reunions, large gatherings and so much more! We have state-of-the-art steel docks that can be rented, as well as a small private beach. We are conveniently located right next to a larger public beach that has a lifeguard and playground. Stunning lake views are available from each and every house and of course, from the incredible roof-top deck. Enjoy amenities such as grills, gas fire pits, lounge chairs, pool table, hot tub, and more during your stay with us. The entire estate is suitable for up to 32 guests.

From October through April, 3 of the 5 houses are available to rent all together or separately. Our "off" season is anything but as the Lake George area offers amazing activities all year round! In the fall, enjoy leaf peeping season for some of the most vibrant fall foliage, or a hike up a local peak for a birds-eye view of the lake. In the winter, we are conveniently located near Gore and West mountains for skiing, snowboarding, and snow tubing. There is also the annual ice castle festival walking distance from the property in the Lake George Village. The spring is a beautiful time to stroll around the Village or Bolton Landing to the north.

We are a family owned and run property since May of 2022 when the Estate was purchased from Bud and Michelle Hochberg. Bud and Michelle lived on and maintained the property for many years and are the reason that it is as amazing as it is today. We are incredibly grateful for their hard work turning the Estate into the perfect get-away destination!

Our family has been vacationing in Lake George for many years and live just an hour to the south. We have three beautiful (almost all adult) children and are incredibly excited to share our small piece of heaven with you! Since we are family run, we are committed to making your stay as comfortable as possible and strive to provide high quality service, care, and attention to all of our guests' needs. Please do not hesitate to contact us for more information or special accommodations.

We hope you are able to make the trip to the Queen of Lakes and trust us with your stay!

Holly and Frank Congiusta Congiusta Arms North LG, LLC
