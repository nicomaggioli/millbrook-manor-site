About This Home

Queens

The largest house on the property, Queens is three stories with spectacular views of the lake. This home has five bedrooms and four bathrooms, comfortably sleeping 10 people. Sunset Escape features dual central heating and air conditioning as well as a washer and dryer.

The top floor features two master ensuite bedrooms, featuring one a king bed and the other a queen. Both suites feature adjustable beds and spectacular views.

The main floor is an open concept with a large living room, TV, kitchen and dining area with more amazing views. Also on the main floor is another bedroom with a queen bed and full bath with standup shower.

The lower level has two additional bedrooms, one with a queen bed, the other with a twin over full bunk bed. Take a step outside the sliding glass doors and enjoy the view and lake breeze!
